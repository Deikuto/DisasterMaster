The Solution

Learn by doing — not by reading.

Disaster Master places you inside the emergency. Make split-second decisions. Face real consequences. Build instincts that last.

01

Educational Focus

Safety rules are woven directly into gameplay. Players learn through actions and observe the direct link between choices and consequences — creating lasting knowledge.

02

Real-World Preparation

Scenarios recreate conditions close to reality. Players practise specific actions in a safe virtual space — finding shelter, using fire extinguishers, evacuating smartly.

03

Pressure-Tested Decisions

Emergencies are sudden, stressful, and fast. Our time-pressured levels train calm reasoning under stress — reducing panic when it matters most.

04

Instant Feedback Loop

The environment reacts to every choice. Visual warnings, real-time indicators, and post-failure guidance help players understand mistakes and correct them immediately.

Survival Scenarios

7 levels. 4 disasters. 0 room for error.

Every level is grounded in official safety guidelines and validated by civil protection experts.

⚡
Disaster 01

Earthquake

L1
Classroom

Books fly off shelves. Lamps swing and fall. You must find cover under a sturdy desk before the shaking brings everything down on you — every second of delay raises your injury risk.

L2
Grocery Store

Shelves topple and products scatter across the floor. Avoid falling objects and reach the exit — getting clear of unstable structures before they collapse.

🔥
Disaster 02

Fire

L3
Kitchen

A pan ignites on the hob. Call 112. Turn off the heat. Use a Class F extinguisher — or smother it with a lid to cut oxygen. Open a window. Then evacuate.

L4
Office Building

Smoke has filled the entire floor. Visibility is near zero. Move low to the ground, below the smoke — and find the emergency exit before the air runs out.

🌊
Disaster 03

Flood

L5
Warehouse

Burst pipes are rapidly flooding the room. Find and shut every damaged valve to stop the rising water — then reach the stairs and escape before you're submerged.

L6
Urban Flash Flood

A tidal surge is swallowing the streets. Navigate around floating debris, submerged cars, and destroyed infrastructure — reach high ground before the current takes you.

How It Works

A system built for real learning.

🎮

Gameplay Module

Controls the core mechanics and level dynamics — environment behaviour, event sequences, success/failure conditions. Every scenario runs on timed pressure with real consequences.

🕹

Player Control Module

First-person control with multiple states: standing, crouching, sprinting, jumping. Each state affects speed and visibility — crouching in smoke is both realistic and mechanically necessary.

📚

Educational Content Module

Delivers real safety guidance at critical moments. On failure, targeted tips explain exactly what went wrong. An Info section links to authoritative external resources so players can go deeper.

🖥

UI Module

Real-time task updates, countdown timers, danger warnings, and audio-visual feedback all synchronised. The interface surfaces danger cues without breaking immersion.

🌍

Localisation Module

Full Bulgarian and English support, built modular — new languages can be added without touching the codebase. Designed for international educational deployment.

⚙

Modular Architecture

Every system is independent. New disasters, levels, and mechanics can be added without rebuilding the foundation — the game is designed to grow.
